Yorkshire Terrier

Yorkshire Terrier are very playful but cautious in nature.They can be able to read human emotions and act appropriately according to the occasion.They do have great defensive mechanisms and antics to keep enemy at bay.They are very obedient but at same very harsh when wronged.They are such a great companion for persons who want a pet that wil always put a smile on their face.
